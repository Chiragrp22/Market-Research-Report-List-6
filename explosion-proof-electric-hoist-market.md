Conversely, wire rope hoists employ a steel wire rope for lifting heavy loads, providing greater strength and durability. Both types are designed to operate safely in explosive atmospheres, ensuring compliance with safety standards and effectively addressing the needs of industries such as oil and gas, mining, and chemical processing.</p></p>
