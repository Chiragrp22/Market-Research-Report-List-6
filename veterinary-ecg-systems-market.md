<p><p>The veterinary ECG systems market is segmented into three main types: Resting ECG, Holter ECG, and Other. Resting ECGs are used for quick assessments, capturing heart activity while the animal is at rest. Holter ECGs offer continuous monitoring over 24 hours or more, allowing for detailed analysis of heart rhythms during normal activities. The “Other” category includes specialized systems for specific diagnostic needs, such as event recorders and telemetry devices, catering to diverse veterinary settings and clinical requirements.</p></p>
