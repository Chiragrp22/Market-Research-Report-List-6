<p><p>The piezo materials market is broadly categorized into soft and hard piezo materials. Soft piezo materials exhibit high piezoelectric coefficients, making them ideal for applications requiring large displacements and high sensitivity, such as sensors and actuators. Conversely, hard piezo materials offer greater mechanical strength and stability, suitable for high-temperature and high-voltage applications, like ultrasonic transducers. Both types play crucial roles in electronics, automotive, and medical devices, fulfilling diverse needs in vibration control and energy harvesting technologies.</p></p>
