<p><p>The automotive rear view monitor market comprises two main types: wireless and wired monitors. Wireless monitors utilize radio frequency or Wi-Fi technology to transmit video signals from the rear camera to the display, offering easier installation with reduced wiring clutter. In contrast, wired monitors use direct cable connections, providing a stable signal and potentially higher video quality, but requiring more complex installation. Both types enhance safety, aiding drivers in parking and reversing by providing a clear view of their surroundings.</p></p>
