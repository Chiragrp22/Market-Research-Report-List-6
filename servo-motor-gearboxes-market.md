<p><p>Servo motor gearboxes are classified into several types, including planetary, spiral bevel, and other variants. </p><p>Planetary servo motor gearboxes utilize a series of gears arranged in a compact design, providing high torque and efficiency, suitable for precision applications. </p><p>Spiral bevel servo motor gearboxes feature angled gears promoting smooth and quiet operation, ideal for applications requiring directional changes. </p><p>Other gearbox types may include worm, helical, or harmonic drives, each offering unique advantages in specific use cases, such as space constraints or load handling.</p></p>
