<p><p>The mmWave substrates market comprises several types essential for high-frequency applications. LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) substrates are known for their excellent thermal stability and low loss, making them ideal for high-performance electronic components. PI (Polyimide) substrates offer flexibility and high-temperature resistance, suitable for advanced circuitry. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) substrates provide superb dielectric properties and minimal signal loss, beneficial for RF applications. Other substrates may include materials like FR-4 and ceramic, catering to specific requirements in the mmWave technology landscape.</p></p>
