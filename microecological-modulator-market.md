<p><p>The Microecological Modulator Market encompasses three primary types: probiotics, prebiotics, and synbiotics. Probiotics are live beneficial bacteria that improve gut health and overall well-being. Prebiotics are non-digestible fibers that promote the growth of these beneficial bacteria in the gut. Synbiotics combine both probiotics and prebiotics to enhance their effects for improved gut microbiota balance. Together, these products support digestive health, boost immunity, and manage various health conditions, driving significant interest in the health and wellness sectors.</p></p>
