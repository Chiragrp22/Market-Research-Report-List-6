<p><p>Grain Oriented Electrical Steel (GOES) is categorized into three market types based on magnetic properties and manufacturing techniques. High Magnetic Strength GOES features enhanced magnetic performance, ideal for applications demanding higher efficiency, like transformers. Conventional GOES offers standard magnetic properties suitable for various electrical devices. Domain Refinement GOES utilizes advanced processing to improve magnetic characteristics, reducing losses and enhancing performance. These types cater to diverse energy applications, optimizing efficiency and performance across the electrical sector.</p></p>
