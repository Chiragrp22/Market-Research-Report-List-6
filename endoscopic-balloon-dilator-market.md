There are two main types of endoscopic balloon dilators: single lumen and double lumen. Single lumen dilators have a single channel through which the balloon inflates, while double lumen dilators have two separate channels, one for inflating the balloon and the other for inserting an endoscope or other instruments. Both types of dilators are commonly used in minimally invasive procedures to treat conditions like strictures or blockages in the gastrointestinal tract.</p></p>
