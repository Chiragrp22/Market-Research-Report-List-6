<p><strong>The Concrete Repair Mortars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer-modified Cementitious Mortar</li><li>Epoxy-based Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Concrete Repair Mortars market features two main types: polymer-modified cementitious mortars and epoxy-based mortars. Polymer-modified cementitious mortars incorporate polymers to enhance adhesion, flexibility, and durability, making them suitable for a variety of repair applications. Epoxy-based mortars, on the other hand, consist of epoxy resins that provide superior bond strength and resistance to chemical exposure, making them ideal for demanding environments. Both types cater to specific repair needs in construction and maintenance, ensuring long-lasting performance.</p></p>
