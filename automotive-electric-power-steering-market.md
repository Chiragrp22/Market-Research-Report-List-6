<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Column Assist EPS</li><li>Single-Pinion Assist EPS</li><li>Dual-Pinion Assist EPS</li><li>Rack Assist EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Column Assist EPS** utilizes a motor attached to the steering column, providing support for maneuverability.</p><p>2. **Single-Pinion Assist EPS** features a single gear set, enhancing responsiveness and simplifying design.</p><p>3. **Dual-Pinion Assist EPS** employs two pinions for improved steering feel and precision.</p><p>4. **Rack Assist EPS** integrates the motor directly into the steering rack, offering compactness and efficient power allocation. Each type caters to different vehicle requirements and driving dynamics.</p></p>
