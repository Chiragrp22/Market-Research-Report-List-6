<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) system market comprises several key components, including hard tags, soft tags, deactivators or detachers, detection systems, and permanent deactivation tags. Hard tags are robust security devices used on high-value items. Soft tags are adhesive labels suitable for retail environments. Deactivators or detachers are tools for removing or disabling tags at checkout. Detection systems consist of sensors that trigger alarms when unauthorized items pass through exits. Permanent deactivation tags provide a secure way to mark items as sold.</p></p>
