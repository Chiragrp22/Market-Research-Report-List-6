<p><ul><li>LLDPE</li><li>LDPE</li><li>HDPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mulch film market is primarily categorized into three types based on the polyethylene used: LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LLDPE offers flexibility and puncture resistance, making it suitable for various crops. LDPE is known for its softness and ease of handling, while HDPE is characterized by its strength and durability, often used in long-lasting applications. Each type caters to specific agricultural needs, enhancing crop yield and soil protection.</p></p>
