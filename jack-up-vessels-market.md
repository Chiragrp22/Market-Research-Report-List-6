<p><p>Jack-up vessels are mobile offshore drilling units used primarily in shallow waters. The market can be divided into self-propelled jack-up vessels and normal jack-up vessels. Self-propelled jack-up vessels are equipped with their own propulsion systems, allowing for easier mobility between locations without relying on tugs. Normal jack-up vessels, on the other hand, require tow vessels for movement. Both types serve the oil and gas industry for drilling and maintenance operations but differ in transport and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
