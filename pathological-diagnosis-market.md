<p><p>The pathological diagnosis market encompasses various subtypes, each focusing on specific aspects of disease identification. Histopathology involves the examination of tissue samples to diagnose diseases like cancer. Cell pathology emphasizes studying cell samples, particularly for cytological examinations. Immunohistochemical pathology uses specific antibodies to detect proteins in tissues, aiding in diagnosing various conditions. Molecular pathology focuses on the genetic and molecular basis of diseases, utilizing techniques like PCR and sequencing to provide precise diagnoses and inform treatment decisions.</p></p>
