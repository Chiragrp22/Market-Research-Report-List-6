<p><p>The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) reader market is primarily divided into fixed and handheld RFID readers. Fixed RFID readers are stationary devices installed at specific locations, such as entry and exit points, to automatically capture tags' data, enhancing inventory management and tracking processes. In contrast, handheld RFID readers are portable devices that allow users to manually scan tags, providing flexibility for tasks like stocktaking or asset tracking in various environments. Both types boost efficiency and accuracy in data collection.</p></p>
