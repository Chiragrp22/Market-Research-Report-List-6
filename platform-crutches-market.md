<p><p>Platform crutches are specialized mobility aids designed to support individuals with limited arm strength or conditions affecting their upper body mobility. Unlike traditional crutches, which exert pressure on the underarm and require significant upper body strength, platform crutches feature a platform or armrest that allows users to support their weight through their forearms. This design enhances comfort and reduces strain, making them particularly beneficial for people with disabilities, injuries, or post-operative recovery needs.</p><p>The Platform Crutches Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.90% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing awareness regarding mobility aids, a rising aging population, and a surge in sports-related injuries requiring rehabilitation. Additionally, advancements in materials and engineering have led to the development of lighter, more durable, and ergonomic crutches, appealing to a broader user base.</p><p>Current trends indicate a shift towards customizable and smart mobility solutions that integrate technology for enhanced usability.
