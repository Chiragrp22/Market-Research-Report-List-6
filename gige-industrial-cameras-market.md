<p><p>The GigE industrial cameras market is primarily divided into two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their high image quality, sensitivity, and low noise levels, making them ideal for applications requiring precise image capture. In contrast, CMOS cameras offer faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and integration capabilities, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Both technologies serve distinct market needs in industries such as manufacturing, automation, and surveillance.</p></p>
