<p><strong>The Mechanical Gripping System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Gripping System</li><li>Centric Gripping System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical gripping system market encompasses various technologies, primarily categorized into parallel and centric gripping systems. Parallel gripping systems feature two jaws that move parallel to each other, ideal for handling flat or cylindrical objects, providing stability and uniform force. In contrast, centric gripping systems have two jaws that move symmetrically towards the center, making them suitable for grasping round or irregularly shaped items, ensuring balanced grip and minimizing slippage. Both systems serve diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
