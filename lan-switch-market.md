<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The LAN Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switching</li><li>Layer 3 Switching</li><li>Layer 4 Switching</li><li>Multi-Layer switching (MLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LAN switch market comprises several types based on their operational layers. Layer 2 switching focuses on Data Link layer functions, dealing primarily with MAC addresses for traffic management. Layer 3 switching adds IP address handling, facilitating routing and inter-VLAN communication. Layer 4 switching considers the Transport layer, allowing for traffic management based on session information. Multi-Layer Switching (MLS) combines these functionalities, offering advanced capabilities for both routing and switching, enhancing performance and flexibility in managing network traffic.</p></p>
