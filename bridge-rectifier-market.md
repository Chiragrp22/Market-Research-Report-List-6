<p><ul><li>Full-Bridge Rectifier</li><li>Half-Bridge Rectifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bridge rectifier market is primarily divided into two types: full-bridge rectifiers and half-bridge rectifiers. Full-bridge rectifiers utilize four diodes to convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC), offering efficient power conversion and higher output voltage, making them ideal for various applications, including power supplies and renewable energy systems. In contrast, half-bridge rectifiers use two diodes, providing simplicity and lower cost, suitable for applications requiring moderate power conversion without the complexity of a full-bridge setup.</p></p>
