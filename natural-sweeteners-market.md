<p><p>The natural sweeteners market is divided into high-intensity and low-intensity segments based on sweetness levels. High-intensity sweeteners, such as stevia or monk fruit, provide significant sweetness with minimal or zero calories, ideal for low-calorie and sugar-free products. In contrast, low-intensity sweeteners, like honey and maple syrup, add sweetness along with calories and flavor, appealing to consumers seeking healthier alternatives while enjoying traditional taste. Both segments cater to health-conscious consumers aiming to reduce sugar intake.</p></p>
