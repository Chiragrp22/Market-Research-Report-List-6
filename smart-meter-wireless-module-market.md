LoRa is known for its long-range capabilities and low power consumption, making it ideal for urban and rural smart metering applications. In contrast, NB-IoT offers better capacity for dense urban areas, providing reliable connectivity for a vast number of devices within a limited spectrum. Both technologies enhance data transmission efficiency, enabling real-time monitoring and management of energy consumption seamlessly.</p></p>
