<p><ul><li>Mechanical Pulping</li><li>Chemical Pulping</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recycled pulp market is categorized into three main types: mechanical pulping, chemical pulping, and others. Mechanical pulping involves physically breaking down fibers, resulting in higher yield but lower quality, suitable for newsprint and packaging. Chemical pulping uses chemicals to dissolve lignin, producing stronger, high-quality pulp for fine papers and cardboard. The "others" category encompasses alternative processes like semi-chemical pulping and deinking, which improve fiber quality and promote recycling. Each method serves distinct applications based on product requirements.</p></p>
