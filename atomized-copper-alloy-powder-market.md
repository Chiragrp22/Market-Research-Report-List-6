<p><ul><li>Water Atomized</li><li>Air Atomized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomized copper alloy powder market is primarily segmented into water atomized and air atomized powders. Water atomized powders are produced by rapidly cooling molten metal with water, resulting in spherical particles with a fine distribution, ideal for applications requiring high density and excellent flow. In contrast, air atomized powders are created by dispersing molten metal in air, leading to irregular particle shapes and larger sizes, suitable for specific applications in casting and additive manufacturing. Both types cater to diverse industries.</p></p>
