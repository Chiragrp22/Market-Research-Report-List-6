<p><strong>The Modular Fabrication Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent</li><li>Relocatable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular fabrication market is divided into two primary types: permanent and relocatable structures. Permanent modular fabrication involves off-site construction of building components that are intended for long-term use at a fixed location, often featuring high-quality materials and designs. In contrast, relocatable modular fabrication comprises units designed for easy transport and assembly, allowing for temporary use or adaptability in various settings. Both types cater to diverse needs, enhancing construction efficiency and meeting varying spatial requirements.</p></p>
