<p><strong>The Medical Pipettes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Pipette</li><li>Multichannel Pipette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical pipettes market is primarily divided into single-channel and multichannel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes allow for precise liquid handling one sample at a time, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy and flexibility. In contrast, multichannel pipettes enable simultaneous dispensing of multiple samples, enhancing throughput and efficiency in high-volume testing environments. Both types cater to various laboratory needs, with their selection based on the specific requirements of the experiment, sample type, and desired precision.</p></p>
