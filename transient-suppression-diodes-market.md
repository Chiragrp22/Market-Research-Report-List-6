<p><p>Transient Suppression Diodes (TSDs) are crucial for protecting electronic circuits from voltage spikes. The market is divided into two main types: Uni-Polar and Bi-Polar TSDs. Uni-Polar TSDs are designed to clamp positive voltage transients, while Bi-Polar TSDs can handle both positive and negative transients, providing comprehensive protection. The choice between the two depends on the specific application needs, voltage levels, and circuit configurations, influencing market trends and demands in various industries, particularly in electronics and automotive sectors.</p></p>
