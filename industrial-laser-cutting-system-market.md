<p><p>The Industrial Laser Cutting System market encompasses various cutting techniques. Laser Vaporization Cutting uses high-energy lasers to vaporize materials, producing precise cuts without significant thermal impact. Laser Melting and Cutting involve melting materials with a laser and then blowing away the molten residue, ideal for metals. Laser Oxygen Cutting utilizes a combination of laser and oxygen, enhancing cutting speed through oxidation. Additionally, "Other" methods may include specific applications like laser engraving or marking, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
