<p><p>The carbon fiber sizing agent market is categorized into two main types: dispersion type and emulsion type. Dispersion type sizing agents involve solid particles suspended in a liquid, providing uniform coating and enhanced bonding properties for carbon fibers. Emulsion type sizing agents consist of oil or resin in water, offering excellent wetting and processing capabilities. Both types are essential for improving the performance, durability, and compatibility of carbon fibers in various applications, including aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods industries.</p></p>
