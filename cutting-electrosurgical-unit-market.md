<p><strong>The Cutting Electrosurgical Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bipolar Electrosurgery</li><li>Monopolar Electrosurgery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cutting electrosurgical unit market is primarily divided into two types: bipolar and monopolar electrosurgery. Bipolar electrosurgery utilizes two active electrodes, allowing precise cutting and coagulation with minimal thermal spread, making it ideal for delicate tissues. In contrast, monopolar electrosurgery involves a single active electrode and a grounding pad, allowing for a broader range of cutting and coagulation but with greater thermal effects on surrounding tissues. Both methods are essential in various surgical procedures, enhancing precision and patient safety.</p></p>
