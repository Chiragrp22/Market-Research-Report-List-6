<p><p>The electro-pneumatic valve positioners market is categorized into single acting and double acting types. Single acting electro-pneumatic valve positioners function using a spring mechanism, requiring air pressure only for the movement in one direction, while springs return the valve to its default position. In contrast, double acting electro-pneumatic valve positioners employ air pressure in both directions, allowing for more precise control and quicker response times. These types cater to various industrial applications, enhancing automation and process efficiency.</p></p>
