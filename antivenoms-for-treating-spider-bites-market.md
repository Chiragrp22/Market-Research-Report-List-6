<p><p>Antivenoms for treating spider bites are specialized medications used to neutralize venom. The market is primarily categorized into two types: polyvalent and monovalent antivenoms. Polyvalent antivenoms are designed to target multiple snake or spider species, offering broader protection against various envenomations. In contrast, monovalent antivenoms are specifically formulated for a particular species, providing a more targeted approach. The choice between these types depends on factors such as the type of spider involved, the severity of the bite, and patient needs.</p></p>
