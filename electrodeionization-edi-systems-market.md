<p><p>Electrodeionization (EDI) systems are water purification technologies that utilize ion-exchange membranes and electrical potential to remove dissolved ions from water. Two primary membrane types are homogeneous and heterogeneous. Homogeneous membranes have uniform structure and properties, leading to consistent performance and easier manufacturing. In contrast, heterogeneous membranes possess distinct layers or phases, providing enhanced selectivity and improved ionic transport. Both types serve various applications, including industrial water treatment, pharmaceutical processes, and food & beverage purification, contributing to efficient water quality management.</p></p>
