<p><strong>The Metal Cutting Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Metal Cutting Fluids</li><li>Semi-Synthetic Metal Cutting Fluids</li><li>Synthesis Metal Cutting Fluids</li><li>Neat Oil Metal Cutting Fluids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Cutting Fluids market comprises various types, each serving specific machining needs. Emulsion Metal Cutting Fluids combine water and oil, providing effective cooling and lubrication. Semi-Synthetic Metal Cutting Fluids offer a balanced blend of synthetic and mineral oils, enhancing performance with reduced residues. Synthetic Metal Cutting Fluids are chemical-based, delivering superior cooling and lubrication without oil. Neat Oil Metal Cutting Fluids are pure oils, primarily used for high-speed machining, offering excellent lubrication and surface finish. These distinctions cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
