<p><strong>The Hexamethylenediamine (HMD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adiponitrile Method</li><li>Hexanediol Method</li><li>Caprolactam Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hexamethylenediamine (HMD) is produced through several methods, each catering to specific industrial needs. The Adiponitrile Method involves the hydrogenation of adiponitrile and is commonly used due to its efficiency and cost-effectiveness. The Hexanediol Method utilizes hexanediol and offers versatility in specific applications. Meanwhile, the Caprolactam Method derives HMD from caprolactam and is notable in the nylon production process. Each method influences HMD's quality, yield, and suitability for various end-use applications in textiles, plastics, and more.</p></p>
