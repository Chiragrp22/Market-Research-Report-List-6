<p><strong>The Plaster Trimmer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Plaster Trimmer</li><li>Dry Plaster Trimmer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plaster trimmer market is segmented into two main types: wet plaster trimmers and dry plaster trimmers. Wet plaster trimmers are designed for use with fresh plaster, enabling smooth finishes and precise shaping during application. They typically feature robust blades that can handle the moisture of wet plaster. In contrast, dry plaster trimmers are used for finishing dried plaster surfaces, allowing for detailing and smoothing once the plaster has set. Both types cater to different stages of plaster application and finishing processes.</p></p>
