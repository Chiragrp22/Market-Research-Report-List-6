<p><p>The panoramic security camera market is primarily divided into two types: IP cameras and analog cameras. IP cameras utilize internet protocols for high-resolution video streaming and remote access, offering advanced features like motion detection and cloud storage. Conversely, analog cameras capture video in a traditional format, typically connected via coaxial cable, making them more straightforward but less flexible. Both types serve different user needs, with IP cameras catering to modern, tech-savvy consumers, while analog cameras appeal to those seeking cost-effective solutions.</p></p>
