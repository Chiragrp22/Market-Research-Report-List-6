<p><strong>The Automatic Power Factor Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Power Factor Controller</li><li>Passive Power Factor Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Power Factor Controller (APFC) market includes two main types: Active Power Factor Controllers and Passive Power Factor Controllers. Active Power Factor Controllers utilize advanced technology and electronic circuitry to dynamically adjust power factor in real-time, improving system efficiency and reducing losses. On the other hand, Passive Power Factor Controllers rely on fixed capacitors or reactors to correct power factor, suitable for specific loads and simpler applications. Together, they enhance electrical system performance and optimize energy consumption across various industries.</p></p>
