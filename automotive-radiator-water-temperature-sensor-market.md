<p><p>The automotive radiator water temperature sensor market is primarily categorized into analog and digital sensors. In contrast, digital sensors convert temperature measurements into a digital signal, allowing for precise data processing and integration with modern vehicle control systems. Both types play critical roles in engine management, enhancing efficiency, performance, and safety by ensuring optimal operating temperatures are maintained.</p></p>
