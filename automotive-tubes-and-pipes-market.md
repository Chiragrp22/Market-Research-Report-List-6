<p><p>The automotive tubes and pipes market is primarily categorized into welded and seamless types. Welded tubes are created by joining flat metal sheets, resulting in a cost-effective option for various applications. They are typically used for exhaust systems and structural components. On the other hand, seamless pipes are formed without joints, offering superior strength and durability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications like fuel lines and hydraulic systems. Both types serve essential roles in modern automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
