<p><p>Wavelength Division Multiplexer (WDM) modules are essential in optical communication, allowing multiple signals to be transmitted simultaneously over a single fiber. The two primary types are Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing (CWDM) and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing (DWDM). CWDM uses wider channel spacing, making it cost-effective for shorter distances and lower data rates, while DWDM offers tighter channel spacing, enabling higher capacity over longer distances. Both technologies enhance network efficiency and bandwidth, catering to different application needs.</p></p>
