<p><p>The wall-mounted dispenser market is categorized into two main types: manual and electric. Manual dispensers require users to operate them physically, typically through a push or pull mechanism, making them cost-effective and easy to maintain. In contrast, electric dispensers automate the dispensing process, often incorporating sensors for touchless operation, enhancing hygiene and convenience. The choice between these types depends on the target environment, user preferences, and budget considerations, impacting their adoption across various sectors like healthcare, hospitality, and public facilities.</p></p>
