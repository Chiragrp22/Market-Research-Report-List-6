<p><p>The Purge Control Solenoid Valve market is categorized into three main types: 2-way, 3-way, and 4-way valves. 2-way valves manage the flow of fluid in one direction, typically used for simple on/off control. 3-way valves facilitate the diversion of flow between two paths, allowing for mixing or splitting functions. 4-way valves, often used in more complex systems, can control multiple flow paths, enabling more versatile operations. Each type serves different applications in automotive, industrial, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
