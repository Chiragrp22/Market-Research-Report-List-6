<p><p>The Disease-Modifying Antirheumatic Drug (DMARD) market includes several categories. Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) reduce inflammation and pain but do not alter disease progression. Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (SAIDs) are effective for acute flare-ups but are not long-term solutions due to potential side effects. Slow-Acting Drugs, such as gold or methotrexate, gradually modify disease progression. Immunological Preparations, including biologics, target specific immune pathways to effectively manage conditions like rheumatoid arthritis, improving patient outcomes and minimizing joint damage.</p></p>
