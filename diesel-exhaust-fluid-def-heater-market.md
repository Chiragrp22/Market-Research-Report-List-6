<p><p>The Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) heater market consists mainly of two types: straight heaters and oval heaters. Straight heaters are designed for direct and efficient heating of DEF, typically featuring a linear configuration for easy integration into various vehicle systems. In contrast, oval heaters offer a compact design that optimizes space utilization, making them suitable for applications where design constraints exist. Both types aim to ensure effective DEF performance by preventing fluid crystallization in cold temperatures, thereby enhancing emissions control systems.</p></p>
