<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Power Factor Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Power Factor Controllers</li><li>Passive Power Factor Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Factor Controllers (PFCs) enhance the efficiency of electrical systems by optimizing power factor. Active Power Factor Controllers utilize electronic components to actively regulate and improve power factor by adjusting reactive power in real-time, making them suitable for dynamic load conditions. In contrast, Passive Power Factor Controllers rely on fixed capacitive components to compensate for reactive power, offering a straightforward, cost-effective solution for steady-state conditions. Together, they cater to diverse industrial and commercial applications, improving energy efficiency and reducing electricity costs.</p></p>
