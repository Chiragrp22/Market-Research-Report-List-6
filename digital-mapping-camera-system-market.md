<p><strong>The Digital Mapping Camera System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Array Scanners (Pushbroom)</li><li>Systems based on Area Arrays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Mapping Camera System market is divided into two main types: Linear Array Scanners (Pushbroom) and Systems based on Area Arrays. Linear Array Scanners capture images using a single line of sensors, scanning the terrain in a continuous manner, which allows for high-resolution imagery and efficient data capture. In contrast, Area Array Systems utilize a grid of sensors to capture images in a single shot, providing broad coverage and faster imagery acquisition, making them suitable for diverse mapping applications.</p></p>
