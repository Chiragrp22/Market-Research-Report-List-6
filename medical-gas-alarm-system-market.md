<p><p>The medical gas alarm system market consists of three key types: Master Alarms, Area Alarms, and Local Alarms. Master Alarms provide centralized monitoring of multiple gas sources, ensuring overall system integrity. Area Alarms focus on specific zones, alerting staff to gas levels or malfunctions within designated spaces. Local Alarms are situated at individual gas outlets, offering immediate notifications for localized issues. Together, these systems enhance patient safety and operational efficiency in healthcare facilities by ensuring timely alerts for gas-related concerns.</p></p>
