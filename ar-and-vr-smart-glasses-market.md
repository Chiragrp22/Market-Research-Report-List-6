<p><p>The AR and VR smart glasses market comprises three main types: Mobile Phone Smart Glasses, Integrated Smart Glasses, and External Smart Glasses. Mobile Phone Smart Glasses leverage a connected smartphone for processing and display, offering portability and convenience. Integrated Smart Glasses house all essential components within the glasses themselves, providing a standalone experience with enhanced functionalities. External Smart Glasses rely on external devices, like computers or consoles, for processing, typically delivering high-quality visuals but lacking the convenience of integration.</p></p>
