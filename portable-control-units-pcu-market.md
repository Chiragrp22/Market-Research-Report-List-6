<p><p>Portable Control Units (PCUs) are versatile devices used for monitoring and controlling various processes in industrial applications. The market for PCUs is categorized primarily into two types based on input sensors: RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) input and thermocouple input. RTD input devices are known for their accuracy and stability in temperature measurements, making them suitable for precise applications. In contrast, thermocouple input devices offer a broader temperature range and faster response times, making them ideal for high-temperature environments and diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
