<p><p>Glasses-free 3D displays utilize various technologies to create depth perception without special eyewear. Light Barrier Technology employs a series of barriers to direct light towards the viewer's eyes. Lenticular Lens Technology uses a series of lenses to refract images, allowing different views based on the viewer's angle. Directional Backlight offers images with depth by controlling the light direction. Direct Imaging directly projects images to create a 3D effect, while "Others" includes emerging technologies that enhance or create 3D visuals without glasses.</p></p>
