<p><p>The snowmelting cables market can be categorized into two main types: constant temperature and temperature-limited systems. Constant temperature cables maintain a consistent heat output, ensuring effective snow and ice melting regardless of outdoor conditions. Conversely, temperature-limited cables are designed to activate only when ambient temperatures drop below a specified threshold, optimizing energy usage and reducing operational costs. Both systems are essential for preventing snow accumulation in various applications, enhancing safety and accessibility in cold climates.</p></p>
