<p><p>The food texturants market encompasses various components that enhance the texture and consistency of food products. Hydrocolloids, such as gelatin and pectin, are water-soluble substances that form gels and thicken liquids. Gums, like guar and xanthan, are used to improve viscosity and stabilizing emulsions. Stabilizers serve to maintain the desired texture and prevent separation in food mixtures. Together, these ingredients play a crucial role in improving mouthfeel, shelf life, and overall quality of food products across various applications.</p></p>
