<p><p>Automated lubrication systems enhance equipment longevity and efficiency by delivering precise amounts of lubricant. The grease lubrication system uses a viscous substance, ideal for high-load applications; it minimizes leakage and requires less frequent maintenance. In contrast, the oil lubrication system circulates thinner fluids, suitable for high-speed machinery, ensuring rapid heat dissipation and effective lubrication. Both systems reduce manual maintenance efforts, improve safety, and optimize performance across various industries, such as manufacturing, automotive, and mining.</p></p>
