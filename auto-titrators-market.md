<p><p>The auto titrators market is categorized into several types, including potential titration and coulomb titration. Potential titration involves measuring the potential difference as a reaction occurs, allowing precise endpoint determination. Coulomb titration, on the other hand, quantifies the amount of analyte based on charge passed in an electrochemical reaction, offering high accuracy. Other types might include various specialized titration methods for specific applications in pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and food safety, enhancing precision and efficiency in titration processes.</p></p>
