<p><p>The industrial abrasives market is divided into two main types: metallic and nonmetallic abrasives. Metallic abrasives include materials such as steel grit and shot, which are primarily used for cleaning and surface preparation due to their durability and effectiveness. Nonmetallic abrasives consist of materials like alumina, garnet, and silica; these are often employed for finer finishing and delicate applications. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and construction, enhancing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
