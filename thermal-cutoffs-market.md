<p><p>The thermal cutoffs market encompasses primarily three types: thermal fuses, thermal switches, and other related components. Thermal fuses are one-time use devices that interrupt circuits when temperatures exceed safe limits, providing overheat protection. Thermal switches, on the other hand, can be reset and control heating elements by switching circuits on and off at predetermined temperatures. The "other" category includes advanced thermal management devices like thermistors and temperature sensors, which enhance safety and efficiency across various applications.</p></p>
