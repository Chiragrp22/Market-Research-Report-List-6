<p><p>The dried whole milk market is divided into two primary types: regular and instant. Regular dried whole milk is processed to remove moisture, allowing for a long shelf life and convenient storage but requiring rehydration before use. In contrast, instant dried whole milk undergoes additional processing to ensure it dissolves quickly in water, making it more convenient for consumers seeking immediate use in recipes or beverages. Both types cater to various culinary applications and consumer preferences, driving market demand.</p></p>
