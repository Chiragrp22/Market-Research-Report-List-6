<p><p>The medical tubular bandage market features primarily two types: tubular elastic support bandages and tubular elastic compression bandages. Tubular elastic support bandages provide gentle support and stabilization for joints and muscles during movement, making them suitable for mild injuries and post-operative recovery. In contrast, tubular elastic compression bandages are designed to apply uniform pressure to reduce swelling and prevent tissue damage in traumatic injuries. Both types play crucial roles in rehabilitation and injury management in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
