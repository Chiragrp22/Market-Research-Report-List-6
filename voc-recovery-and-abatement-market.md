<p><p>The VOC recovery and abatement market encompasses various technologies to manage volatile organic compounds. Oxidizers reduce VOC emissions through combustion, while filtration systems trap contaminants. Membrane separations utilize selective permeability to separate VOCs from gases. Scrubbers and strippers remove VOCs from air or liquid streams using solvents or chemical reactions. Condensation techniques cool vapors to capture VOCs as liquids. Adsorbent systems use materials to capture and hold VOCs, facilitating their recovery and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
