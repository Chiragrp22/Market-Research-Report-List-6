<p><p>The glass insulators market primarily comprises two main types: suspension glass insulators and pin glass insulators. Suspension glass insulators are employed in overhead power lines, supporting conductors while providing electrical insulation and durability against environmental factors. They are typically mounted on towers and are crucial for long-distance transmission. In contrast, pin glass insulators are used in lower voltage distribution systems, mounted on poles to secure the wire. Both types play essential roles in enhancing electrical grid reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
