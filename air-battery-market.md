<p><p>The air battery market encompasses three main types: non-rechargeable, rechargeable, and fuel cells. Non-rechargeable air batteries are single-use, converting chemical energy to electricity until depleted. Rechargeable air batteries allow for multiple cycles of charging and discharging, enhancing efficiency and sustainability. Fuel cells, another type, generate electricity through a chemical reaction between hydrogen and oxygen, emitting only water as a byproduct. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, contributing to advancements in clean energy and power storage solutions.</p></p>
