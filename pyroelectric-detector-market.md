<p><p>The pyroelectric detector market is primarily segmented into three types: DLaTGS (Deuterated L-Alanine Doped Tri-Glycine Sulfate), LiTaO3 (Lithium Tantalate), and PZT (Lead Zirconate Titanate). DLaTGS is popular for its sensitivity and stability in thermal detection applications. LiTaO3 is favored for its high electro-optic properties and wide spectral response. PZT is known for its robust performance and efficiency in converting thermal energy to electric signals. Each type has distinct advantages tailored to specific sensor applications.</p></p>
