<p><p>Clean Room Technology market offers various types of products to ensure a controlled environment in sensitive settings. Fan Filter Units (FFU) maintain air quality by filtering out contaminants. HVAC Systems regulate temperature and humidity levels. Laminar Air Flow Systems create a unidirectional airflow to prevent particle contamination. Biosafety Cabinets provide a sterile workspace for handling hazardous materials. HEPA Filters trap tiny particles, ensuring clean air. Air Diffusers and Showers distribute filtered air evenly throughout the room. Each of these products plays a crucial role in maintaining a sterile and controlled environment.</p></p>
