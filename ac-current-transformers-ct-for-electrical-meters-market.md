<p><p>AC Current transformers (CTs) are essential components in electrical metering that measure alternating current by stepping down high currents to a lower, manageable level. In the market, CTs are categorized into two main types: pin-type and wire-type. Pin-type CTs are designed for direct connection to circuit boards, offering robust accuracy in readings, while wire-type CTs feature open-loop designs for easy installation around existing conductors. Both types ensure precise current measurement, enhancing the efficiency and reliability of electrical systems.</p></p>
