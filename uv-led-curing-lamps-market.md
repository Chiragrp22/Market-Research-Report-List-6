<p><p>UV LED curing lamps are commonly categorized into three types based on their light source: surface light source lamps, line light source lamps, and others. Surface light source lamps emit UV light evenly across a flat surface, making them suitable for curing larger areas. Line light source lamps emit light in a linear pattern, ideal for curing narrow or specific areas. Other types of UV LED curing lamps may include spot light source lamps or customized lamps with unique designs tailored to specific curing needs.</p></p>
