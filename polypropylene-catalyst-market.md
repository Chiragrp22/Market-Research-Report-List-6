<p><ul><li>Ziegler-Natta Catalyst</li><li>Metallocene Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene catalyst market is primarily divided into two types: Ziegler-Natta catalysts and metallocene catalysts. Ziegler-Natta catalysts, composed of transition metals and alkyl aluminum compounds, are known for their ability to produce a range of polymer properties and are widely used in traditional polypropylene production. Metallocene catalysts, on the other hand, offer enhanced control over polymer architecture, leading to materials with superior properties such as clarity and strength. These distinctions drive innovation and application in various industries.</p></p>
