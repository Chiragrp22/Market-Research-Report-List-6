<p><p>The manual transfer switch market is primarily divided into panel-mounted and din rail-mounted types. Panel-mounted switches are integrated directly into control panels, offering a compact solution for residential and commercial applications, facilitating easy transfer between utility and generator power. Din rail-mounted switches, on the other hand, are designed for installation on standard din rails, commonly used in industrial settings. They provide flexibility in installation and maintenance, supporting modular configurations while ensuring reliable power management in various settings.</p></p>
