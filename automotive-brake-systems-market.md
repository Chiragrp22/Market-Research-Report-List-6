<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Brake Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antilock Braking System (ABS)</li><li>Traction Control System (TCS)</li><li>Electronic Stability Control (ESC)</li><li>Electronic Brake-Force Distribution (EBD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive brake systems market includes several key technologies. The Antilock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock-up during braking, enhancing control. The Traction Control System (TCS) manages wheel spin during acceleration for improved traction. Electronic Stability Control (ESC) helps maintain vehicle stability by detecting and reducing skids. Electronic Brake-Force Distribution (EBD) optimizes brake force between front and rear wheels based on load conditions, enhancing stopping power and safety. Together, these systems improve vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
