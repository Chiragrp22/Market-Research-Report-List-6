<p><ul><li>Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs)</li><li>Power Integrated Modules (PIMs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Advanced Power Modules for the industrial market include Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) and Power Integrated Modules (PIMs). IPMs combine power transistors, drivers, and protection circuits in a single package, enhancing efficiency and simplifying design for motor control applications. PIMs, on the other hand, integrate power components such as IGBTs or MOSFETs with associated circuitry, providing high power density and reduced size. Both modules enable improved thermal performance and reliability, making them ideal for demanding industrial environments.</p></p>
