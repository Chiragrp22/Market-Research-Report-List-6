<p><p>HIC substrates, or high thermal conductivity substrates, are vital in electronic packaging and semiconductor applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: Al2O3 HIC substrates and AlN HIC substrates. Al2O3 (alumina) substrates are widely used due to their good electrical insulation and lower cost, while AlN (aluminum nitride) substrates offer superior thermal conductivity and are preferred for high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on thermal management requirements and manufacturing processes in various industries.</p></p>
