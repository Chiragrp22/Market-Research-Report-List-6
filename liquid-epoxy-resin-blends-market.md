These blends are commonly used in industries such as electronics, construction, and automotive. On the other hand, high viscosity Liquid Epoxy Resin blends have a thicker consistency, making them suitable for applications where gap filling, structural bonding, and reinforcement are required. They are often used in aerospace, marine, and wind energy industries.</p></p>
