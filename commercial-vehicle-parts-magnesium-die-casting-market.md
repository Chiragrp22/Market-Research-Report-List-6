<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Parts Magnesium Die Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Gravity Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Parts Magnesium Die Casting market encompasses several casting methods, each suited for specific applications. Pressure Die Casting utilizes high pressure to inject molten magnesium into molds, producing complex shapes with excellent surface finish. Vacuum Die Casting removes air from the mold, minimizing defects and enhancing part quality. Squeeze Die Casting applies pressure during solidification, ensuring dense and strong components. Gravity Die Casting relies on gravity to fill the mold, offering a cost-effective method for larger parts with simpler geometries.</p></p>
