<p><strong>The Microfluidic Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circular Microfluidic Connectors</li><li>Linear Microfluidic Connectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microfluidic connectors are used to allow the connection of different microfluidic devices or systems. Circular microfluidic connectors are designed in a circular shape to ensure a secure and leak-proof connection between devices, making them ideal for applications requiring continuous flow. Linear microfluidic connectors, on the other hand, are designed in a linear shape and are commonly used for applications where multiple connections need to be made in a straight line. Both types of connectors play a crucial role in enabling the efficient transfer of fluids in microfluidic systems.</p></p>
