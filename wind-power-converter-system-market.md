<p><strong>The Wind Power Converter System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Doubly-Fed</li><li>Full Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind power converter system market primarily consists of two types: doubly-fed and full power converters. Doubly-fed systems utilize a wound rotor induction generator, allowing for variable speed operation while connecting to the grid through a semi-converter arrangement, enabling efficient energy capture and grid support. Full power converters, on the other hand, convert all generated power to the grid frequency, offering enhanced control and grid stability but at a higher cost. Both types play critical roles in wind energy integration.</p></p>
