<p><p>The electrical explosion-proof equipment market encompasses various types designed to prevent ignition in hazardous environments. Flame-proof type involves sturdy enclosures that contain explosions. Increased safety type reduces risk by eliminating possible ignition sources. Intrinsic safety type uses barriers to ensure circuits remain safe under fault conditions. Positive-pressure type maintains inert gas pressure to prevent flammable gases from entering. Oil-immersed type relies on oil to suppress potential sparks, while sand-filled type uses sand to absorb explosions, offering diverse safety solutions across industries.</p></p>
