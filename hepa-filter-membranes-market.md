<p><strong>The HEPA Filter Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PTFE Membrane</li><li>Microglass Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HEPA filter membranes market primarily includes two types: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) membranes and microglass membranes. PTFE membranes are known for their chemical resistance and durability, making them suitable for harsh environments. They efficiently capture fine particles and offer low pressure drop, enhancing energy efficiency. Microglass membranes, on the other hand, are made from glass fibers and provide high filtration efficiency and reliability. They are commonly used in various applications, including cleanrooms and industrial settings, due to their excellent particulate retention capabilities.</p></p>
