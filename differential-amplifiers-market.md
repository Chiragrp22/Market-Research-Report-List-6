<p><ul><li>SMD</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential amplifiers are integral components in various electronic applications, categorized primarily into Surface Mount Device (SMD) and Through Hole types. SMD differential amplifiers are compact and suitable for modern PCB designs, allowing for high-density layouts and automated assembly processes. In contrast, Through Hole differential amplifiers offer robust connections and are often favored in applications requiring increased durability and easier manual soldering. The choice between these types largely depends on application requirements, manufacturing processes, and performance criteria.</p></p>
