<p><p>The glass fibre veil market is primarily categorized into two processes: wet netting and dry netting. </p><p>The wet netting process involves saturating the glass fiber with resin, allowing for better adhesion and a smoother finish, making it ideal for applications requiring a higher level of durability. </p><p>In contrast, the dry netting process uses dry glass fibers laid in a specific pattern, which are then infused with resin, resulting in a lightweight and versatile product suited for various manufacturing needs. Both methods serve distinct industrial applications.</p></p>
