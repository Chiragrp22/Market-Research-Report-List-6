<p><strong>The Switchable Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic</li><li>Thermochromic</li><li>SPD</li><li>PDLC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The switchable glass market consists of various types that adjust their transparency based on external stimuli. Electrochromic glass changes tint when an electric charge is applied, enhancing energy efficiency and privacy. Thermochromic glass reacts to temperature changes, altering its opacity with heat. SPD (Suspended Particle Device) glass offers rapid transparency changes through an electric signal, providing privacy at will. PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) glass uses electric fields to switch from opaque to clear, offering versatile applications in architecture and automotive sectors.</p></p>
