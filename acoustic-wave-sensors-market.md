<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave Sensor</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acoustic Wave Sensors market is primarily divided into two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensors. SAW sensors utilize acoustic waves traveling along the surface of a material, making them suitable for applications in telecommunications and environmental monitoring due to their sensitivity and compact size. Conversely, BAW sensors employ acoustic waves that propagate through the bulk of the material, offering advantages in frequency stability and power handling, thus being favored in industrial and medical applications.</p></p>
