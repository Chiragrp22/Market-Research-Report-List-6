<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Thin-Film Optical Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bandpass Filters</li><li>Notch Filters</li><li>Shortpass Edge Filters</li><li>Longpass Edge Filters</li><li>Dichroic Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thin-film optical filters are designed to selectively transmit or reflect specific wavelengths of light, catering to diverse applications in optics and imaging. Bandpass filters allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass while blocking others. Notch filters reject a narrow band of wavelengths. Shortpass edge filters transmit wavelengths shorter than a specified cutoff and block longer wavelengths, whereas longpass edge filters do the opposite, transmitting longer wavelengths. Dichroic filters selectively reflect certain wavelengths while transmitting others, crucial in multi-wavelength applications.</p></p>
