<p><p>The Platinum RTD temperature sensors market primarily features two types: PT100 and PT1000. PT100 sensors have a resistance of 100 ohms at 0°C, offering high accuracy and stability for various industrial applications. PT1000 sensors, with a resistance of 1000 ohms at 0°C, provide greater sensitivity, making them suitable for applications requiring precise temperature measurement over a wider range. Both sensor types are favored in sectors like manufacturing, HVAC, and environmental monitoring due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
