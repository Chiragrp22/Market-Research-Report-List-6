<p><strong>The Automotive Cooling Fan Control Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Cooling Fan Control Module</li><li>Electric Cooling Fan Control Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cooling fan control module market is divided into two primary types: mechanical and electric cooling fan control modules. Mechanical cooling fan control modules typically involve a fan driven directly by the engine, using a viscous coupling mechanism to adjust speed based on engine temperature. In contrast, electric cooling fan control modules use electric motors to operate fans independently of the engine, allowing for more efficient cooling and improved fuel economy. Both types play vital roles in maintaining optimal engine temperature and performance.</p></p>
