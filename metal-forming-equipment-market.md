<p><ul><li>Bulk Forming</li><li>Sheet Forming</li><li>Powder Metal Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal forming equipment market is divided into three primary types: bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. Bulk forming involves shaping large metal masses using processes like forging and extrusion, focusing on strength and integrity. Sheet forming deals with thin metal sheets through techniques like stamping and bending, emphasizing precision and surface finish. Powder metal forming utilizes fine metal powders compacted and sintered to create complex shapes, offering efficient material use and tailored properties for various applications.</p></p>
