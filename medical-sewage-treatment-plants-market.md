<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Medical Sewage Treatment Plants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Preliminary Stage or Pretreatment</li><li>Primary Treatment Stage</li><li>Secondary Treatment Stage</li><li>Tertiary or Advanced Treatment Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical sewage treatment plants are categorized into four key stages. The preliminary stage, or pretreatment, involves the removal of large solids and debris. The primary treatment stage focuses on sedimentation to separate solids from liquids. In the secondary treatment stage, biological processes break down organic matter, enhancing wastewater quality. Lastly, the tertiary or advanced treatment stage employs advanced filtration and disinfection methods to further purify the effluent, ensuring it meets stringent environmental standards for safe discharge or reuse.</p></p>
