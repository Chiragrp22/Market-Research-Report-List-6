<p><p>The coin cell market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Lithium coin cells offer high energy density and long shelf life, ideal for electronics. Silver coin cells provide stable voltage and are commonly used in high-drain devices like watches. Alkaline coin cells, utilizing manganese dioxide, are cost-effective for low-drain applications. Zinc-air coin cells, often used in hearing aids, provide high capacity through a chemical reaction with oxygen. Other types include specialized cells for niche applications, enhancing overall market diversity.</p></p>
