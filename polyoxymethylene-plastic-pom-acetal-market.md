<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM), commonly known as acetal, is a high-performance engineering plastic widely used in various industries. The market for POM is divided into two main types: homopolymer POM and copolymer POM. Homopolymer POM offers superior mechanical properties, rigidity, and low friction, making it ideal for precision parts in automotive and electronics applications. In contrast, copolymer POM features enhanced chemical resistance and improved performance under harsh environmental conditions, making it suitable for applications in packaging and industrial components.</p></p>
