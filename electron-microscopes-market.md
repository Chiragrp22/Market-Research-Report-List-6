<p><strong>The Electron Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs)</li><li>Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron microscope market is primarily divided into two types: Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs) and Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs). SEMs provide detailed three-dimensional images by scanning a sample's surface with focused electron beams, making them ideal for materials science and biology applications. In contrast, TEMs transmit electrons through ultra-thin samples, revealing intricate internal structures at much higher resolutions, crucial for nanotechnology and semiconductor research. Both types are integral for advanced scientific analysis and industrial inspections.</p></p>
