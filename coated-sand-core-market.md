<p><p>The coated sand core market is primarily divided into two methods: cold and thermal. The cold method involves using a resin-coated sand mixture that cures at room temperature, offering cost-effective production and flexibility. In contrast, the thermal method requires heat to cure the resin, resulting in stronger and more durable cores, suitable for high-performance applications. Both methods cater to diverse industry needs, including automotive and aerospace, providing tailored solutions in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
