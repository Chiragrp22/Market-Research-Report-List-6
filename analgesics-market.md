<p><p>The analgesics market is primarily categorized into two types: Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) and Central Pain Killers. NSAIDs, such as ibuprofen and aspirin, alleviate pain and inflammation by inhibiting specific enzymes. They are commonly used for mild to moderate pain relief. Central Pain Killers, including opioids like morphine and codeine, act on the central nervous system to relieve severe pain. These medications are typically prescribed for acute or chronic pain management, providing essential relief in various medical conditions.</p></p>
