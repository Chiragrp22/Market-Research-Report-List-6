<p><p>The Direct Bonded Copper (DBC) substrate market is characterized by two primary types: AlN DBC ceramic substrates and Al2O3 DBC ceramic substrates. AlN DBC substrates are known for their high thermal conductivity, making them suitable for high-temperature and high-power applications. In contrast, Al2O3 DBC substrates, while offering lower thermal conductivity than AlN, are more cost-effective and widely used in general electronic applications. Both types are integral in enhancing the performance and reliability of electronic devices by providing effective heat dissipation.</p></p>
