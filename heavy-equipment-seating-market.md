<p><ul><li>Mechanical Suspension Seats</li><li>Air Suspension Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy equipment seating market primarily consists of two types: mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats use springs and dampers to absorb shock and vibrations, providing basic comfort and support. In contrast, air suspension seats utilize air bladders to offer adjustable cushioning and enhanced ride quality, adapting to the operator's weight and movement. Both types play a crucial role in reducing operator fatigue and enhancing productivity in demanding environments, ensuring optimal performance in heavy machinery operations.</p></p>
