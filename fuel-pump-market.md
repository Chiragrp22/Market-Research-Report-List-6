<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fuel Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Common Fuel Pump</li><li>GDI Fuel Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel pump market can be categorized primarily into common fuel pumps and GDI (Gasoline Direct Injection) fuel pumps. Common fuel pumps are typically used in traditional internal combustion engines, delivering fuel from the tank to the engine at a consistent pressure. In contrast, GDI fuel pumps serve modern high-efficiency engines by injecting fuel directly into the combustion chamber, allowing for better fuel atomization and improved performance. Both types are essential for meeting varying automotive performance and efficiency standards.</p></p>
