<p><ul><li>Plunger</li><li>Panel Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enclosed switches come in various types, including plunger switches and panel mount switches. Plunger switches are designed with a spring-loaded plunger that activates the switch when pressed down. These switches are commonly used in applications where a physical force needs to be applied to activate the switch. On the other hand, panel mount switches are designed to be mounted directly onto a control panel or enclosure, making them ideal for applications where space is limited or where a sleek, integrated appearance is desired.</p></p>
