<p><p>The direct drive pumps market includes various types designed for different applications. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotating impellers to move fluids, making them efficient for high flow rates. Positive-displacement pumps, in contrast, operate by trapping a fixed volume of fluid and forcing it through the discharge, suitable for high-viscosity liquids. Other types may include gear pumps, diaphragm pumps, and screw pumps, each offering unique benefits for specific industries such as water treatment, oil and gas, and chemical processing.</p></p>
