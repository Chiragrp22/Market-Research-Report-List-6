<p><ul><li>Digital Output Optical Proximity Sensors</li><li>Analog Output Optical Proximity Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical proximity sensors market is segmented into two main types: digital output and analog output sensors. Digital output optical proximity sensors provide a binary signal, indicating whether an object is detected or not, making them ideal for simple on/off applications. In contrast, analog output optical proximity sensors deliver a variable signal, reflecting the distance or presence of an object more precisely, which is suitable for applications requiring fine measurement or continuous monitoring. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing automation and precision.</p></p>
