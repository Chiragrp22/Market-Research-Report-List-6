<p><p>Safety fistula needles are a crucial component of hemodialysis treatment, used to access a patient's blood flow for filtering during dialysis. There are two main types of safety fistula needles: standard fistula needles and AV fistula needles. Standard fistula needles are commonly used for standard dialysis procedures, while AV fistula needles are specifically designed for arteriovenous (AV) fistulas, which are created surgically to provide reliable access for dialysis treatment. Both types of safety fistula needles play a vital role in ensuring the safety and effectiveness of hemodialysis procedures.</p></p>
