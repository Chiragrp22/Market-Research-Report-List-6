<p><p>The heat shield material market comprises three main types: rigid, flexible, and textile heat shield materials. Rigid heat shields are typically made from hard, insulating materials that provide structural support and high-temperature resistance, making them ideal for fixed applications. Flexible heat shields offer adaptability and ease of installation, often using composites or layered materials to withstand thermal exposure in dynamic environments. Textile heat shields utilize specialized fabrics designed to reflect heat, providing lightweight and flexible solutions for various applications, particularly in automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
