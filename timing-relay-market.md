<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Timing Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-delay</li><li>Off-delay</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The timing relay market is categorized into three main types: on-delay, off-delay, and others. On-delay relays activate an output after a preset delay, commonly used in motor control applications to prevent inrush currents. Off-delay relays, conversely, maintain output for a set time after deactivation, aiding in processes like cooling fans. The "others" category encompasses various specialized relays, including repeat cycle and interval timers, catering to niche applications across industries such as manufacturing, HVAC, and automation.</p></p>
