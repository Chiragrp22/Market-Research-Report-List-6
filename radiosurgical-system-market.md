<p><p>The radiosurgical system market comprises several types, including Gamma Knife, Linear Accelerator Based Therapy (LINAC), and Proton Beam Therapy. Gamma Knife is highly effective for treating small brain lesions with precision. LINAC uses high-energy X-rays for various tumors across the body. Proton Beam Therapy employs protons to target tumors with minimal damage to surrounding tissues, making it ideal for sensitive areas. Other emerging technologies, like CyberKnife and stereotactic radiosurgery devices, are also included in this diverse market landscape.</p></p>
