<p><strong>The Mortar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Mixed Mortar</li><li>Dry Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mortar market is divided into two primary types: wet mixed mortar and dry mortar. Wet mixed mortar is prepared on-site, incorporating water with sand, cement, and additives, and is usually applied immediately due to its perishable nature. In contrast, dry mortar is pre-mixed and packaged for convenience, requiring only the addition of water before application. This product offers improved consistency, longer shelf life, and ease of use, making it popular for construction projects where efficiency and quality are essential.</p></p>
