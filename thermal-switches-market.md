<p><ul><li>Thermal Reed Switches</li><li>Mercury Switches</li><li>Rod and Tube Thermal Switches</li><li>Gas-Actuated Thermal Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal switches market encompasses various types, including thermal reed switches, which use magnetic fields for activation; mercury switches, which rely on the movement of mercury to open or close a circuit; rod and tube thermal switches, characterized by their bimetallic components that respond to temperature changes; and gas-actuated thermal switches, utilizing gas expansion to trigger operation. Each type offers unique advantages suited for specific applications, enhancing safety and temperature control across industries like automotive, HVAC, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
