<p><p>The military shoes market comprises three main types: composite toe, soft toe, and steel toe footwear. Composite toe shoes are lightweight and non-metallic, offering protection without adding bulk, making them popular in environments with metal detectors. Soft toe shoes provide comfort and flexibility but lack significant impact protection, suitable for less hazardous areas. Steel toe shoes offer robust protection with a steel reinforcement, essential for high-risk environments where heavy objects may fall, ensuring safety and durability for military personnel.</p></p>
