<p><p>The LAN as a Service (LANaaS) market encompasses three main types: Managed LANaaS, Wi-Fi Only, and Management Only. Managed LANaaS provides end-to-end network management, including hardware and support, enabling organizations to outsource their entire LAN infrastructure. Wi-Fi Only focuses solely on wireless network management, catering to businesses that require high-performance wireless connectivity without extensive wired infrastructure. Management Only services offer network oversight and optimization without providing the physical infrastructure, allowing companies to retain their existing LAN components while enhancing management capabilities.</p></p>
