<p><p>The hernia surgical mesh implants market is divided into two main types: non-absorbable and absorbable mesh implants. Non-absorbable mesh implants are designed for permanent implantation, providing long-term support to the surrounding tissue and reducing the risk of recurrence. Absorbable mesh implants, on the other hand, gradually dissolve over time and are intended for temporary support as the tissue heals. Each type has distinct applications and benefits, influencing surgeon preference and patient outcomes in hernia repair procedures.</p></p>
