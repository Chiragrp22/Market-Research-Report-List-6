<p><p>Residential wall-hung boilers are categorized into two main types: regular wall-hung boilers and condensing wall-hung boilers. Regular wall-hung boilers provide traditional heating by burning fuel, often without maximizing energy efficiency. In contrast, condensing wall-hung boilers utilize advanced technology to reclaim heat from flue gases, significantly improving energy efficiency and reducing emissions. This makes condensing models more environmentally friendly and cost-effective over time while catering to increasing consumer demand for sustainable heating solutions in residential applications.</p></p>
