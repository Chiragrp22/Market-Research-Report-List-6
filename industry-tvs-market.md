<p><strong>The Industry TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression) market is divided into uni-polar and bi-polar types based on their voltage characteristics. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect against positive voltage transients, typically utilized in circuits with a positive ground reference. In contrast, bi-polar TVS devices can handle both positive and negative transients, making them suitable for applications with alternating voltage levels. This differentiation allows for tailored protection strategies in various electronic systems, enhancing equipment reliability and longevity.</p></p>
