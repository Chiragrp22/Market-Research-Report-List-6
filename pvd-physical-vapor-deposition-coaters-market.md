<p><p>The PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coaters market includes various types of equipment such as evaporation and sputtering systems, alongside other methods. Evaporation equipment utilizes thermal energy to vaporize materials, depositing thin films onto substrates for applications like optics and electronics. Sputtering equipment, on the other hand, ejects atoms from a target material through ion bombardment, allowing precise control of film thickness and composition. Other types may include hybrid systems or advanced techniques, expanding the range of applications across industries like semiconductor, automotive, and decorative coatings.</p></p>
