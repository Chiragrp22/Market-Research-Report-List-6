The market can be divided into two main types: cellulose diacetate and cellulose triacetate. Cellulose diacetate, known for its transparency and low hygroscopicity, is commonly used in the production of cigarette filters and photographic films. In contrast, cellulose triacetate offers superior thermal stability and is often utilized in the manufacturing of optical films and high-performance products, catering to a wide range of industrial needs.</p></p>
