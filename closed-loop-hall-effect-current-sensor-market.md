<p><p>The Closed Loop Hall Effect Current Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Linear Output and Threshold Output. Linear Output sensors provide a continuous and proportional voltage output relative to the current passed through, allowing for precise measurement across a range of values. In contrast, Threshold Output sensors activate when the current exceeds a predetermined level, offering discrete signals that indicate specific conditions. Both types are crucial in various applications, including industrial automation, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
