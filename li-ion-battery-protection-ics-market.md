<p><p>The Li-ion battery protection IC market is segmented into single-cell and multi-cell categories. Single-cell protection ICs focus on safeguarding individual lithium-ion cells, ensuring overcharge, over-discharge, and short-circuit protection. In contrast, multi-cell protection ICs manage multiple cells in series, optimizing performance and safety across battery packs used in electric vehicles and larger devices. The choice between these types depends on application requirements, with single-cell often suited for smaller devices, while multi-cell is ideal for larger battery systems.</p></p>
