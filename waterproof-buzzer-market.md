<p><p>The waterproof buzzer market is segmented into three main types: piezoelectric buzzers, magnetic buzzers, and electromagnetic buzzers. Piezoelectric buzzers utilize piezoelectric materials to produce sound when an electric voltage is applied, favored for their compact size and low power consumption. Magnetic buzzers rely on a magnetic field to generate sound and are known for their efficiency. Electromagnetic buzzers utilize an electromagnetic coil to create sound waves, offering robust performance. Each type serves distinct applications in environments exposed to moisture and water.</p></p>
