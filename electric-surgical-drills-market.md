<p><p>The electric surgical drills market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless drills. Wired type drills typically offer consistent power and reliability, making them ideal for prolonged surgeries. They connect directly to a power source but can limit maneuverability. Each type has its advantages, catering to different surgeon preferences and specific surgical needs.</p></p>
