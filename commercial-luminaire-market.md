<p><p>The commercial luminaire market comprises various types of lighting solutions tailored for different applications. LED luminaires are energy-efficient and long-lasting, making them increasingly popular. CFL luminaires offer compact fluorescent lighting options, while LFL luminaires provide linear fluorescent solutions for broader areas. HID luminaires, utilizing high-intensity discharge technology, are ideal for large spaces needing bright illumination. Lastly, halogen luminaires deliver high-quality, focused light but consume more energy, making them less favored in modern applications.</p></p>
