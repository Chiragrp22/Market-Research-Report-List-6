<p><p>The model train market comprises primarily two types: DC Current Two Rails and AC Current Three Rails systems. DC current two-rail systems utilize two tracks for power delivery, with a straightforward design that allows for easy operations and control, catering to both hobbyists and newcomers. In contrast, AC current three-rail systems, often associated with larger brands, feature a third rail that provides power, allowing for advanced features such as more reliable operation and easier train handling on complex layouts.</p></p>
