<p><p>The Turbo Exhaust Valves market is categorized primarily into two types: flange-mounted and welded valves. Flange-mounted valves feature attachment points that allow easy installation and removal, making them ideal for maintenance. In contrast, welded valves are permanently fixed, providing enhanced durability and sealing against leaks, suitable for high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct needs, with flange valves favored for their flexibility in servicing, while welded valves are preferred for their structural integrity and superior performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
