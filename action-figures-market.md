<p><p>The action figures market can be categorized into three primary types: plastic, silica rubber, and others. Plastic figures dominate the market due to their durability and affordability, often featuring detailed designs and various articulations. Silica rubber figures offer a more flexible and softer texture, appealing to collectors who favor realism and poseability. The 'others' category encompasses figures made from materials like resin, metal, or hybrid composites, catering to niche markets and collectors seeking unique or high-end products.</p></p>
